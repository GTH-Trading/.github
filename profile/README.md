# GTH Trading

> Research-driven analytical investing. Structured options. Risk-managed trading.

GTH Trading is a community focused on helping investors and traders make better decisions through technical analysis, options strategies, market research, and disciplined risk management.

The goal is not to predict the future. The goal is to consistently identify favorable opportunities, manage risk, and compound capital over time.

---

## What We Do

### Weekly Blueprint

The Weekly Blueprint is the flagship publication of GTH Trading. Published every weekend, the Blueprint provides:

* Market outlook, including a look at earnings, macroeconomics, geopolitical status, and other market influencers
* Technical analysis of key market sectors
* Stock picks for the upcoming week
* Options trade structures and strike selections
* Risk management considerations

The Blueprint is designed to prepare members for the week ahead before markets open on Monday.

---

### Trading Alerts

The proprietary GTH Trading Alerting System scans the market for bullish technical structures based on the same analytical framework used in manual research.
Current alert criteria include:
* Multi-timeframe analysis
* Multi-indicator analysis
* Trend analysis
* Momentum confirmation
* Regression-based trend evaluation

Future development includes:
* Local bottom identification alerts
* Crypto market alerts
* Enhanced market breadth analysis

The objective is to generate useful, impactful notifications built around extensive analysis, and capable of actional trade scenarios.

---

### Options Education

Many investors understand stocks but never develop a framework for options. GTH Trading provides a practical education on:
* Standard calls and puts
* Straddles/strangles, collars, debit/credit spreads, ratio spreads, calendar spreads, butterflies and condors, and stock repair strategies.
* Risk Management
* Assignment and Exercise Dynamics

The focus is on understanding both opportunity and risk.

---

### Live Trading

Members receive access to live market discussion throughout the trading day. Topics include:
* Market structure
* Earnings reactions
* Technical setups and executions
* Risk assessment/management

Real-time discussion provides context that is difficult to capture in static articles.

## Philosophy

### Cognitive Fitness > Business Velocity

Artificial intelligence is an incredible tool. However, outsourcing all thinking is not the objective.
The Weekly Blueprint, research process, market analysis, and educational content are developed through deliberate analysis and critical thinking.
Just as modern society requires physical exercise despite abundant food, today's environment increasingly requires deliberate cognitive exercise
despite abundant automation.

Maintaining a strong mind is a competitive advantage.

Strategies evolve.
